Privacy Policy

This budgeting application (“ActualBudget”) is a personal finance tool used to track income and expenses.

The App connects to bank accounts through Enable Banking to retrieve transaction data.

We do not sell, share, or distribute any personal data to third parties.

Data We Collect
Bank transaction data (read-only access via Enable Banking)
Account balances
User-created categories and budget data
How Data Is Used

All data is used solely to:

Display and categorise personal financial information
Help users manage their personal budget
Data Storage

Data is stored securely in the App’s database and is only accessible to the user.

Third-Party Services

We use Enable Banking to securely connect to financial institutions. Their privacy policy applies to bank data access.
